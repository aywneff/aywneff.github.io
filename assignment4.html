In this circuit the photoresistor funtions as an input and the LED strip serves as an output. The photoresistor is controlled by pin A0 and the LED strip is controlled by pin 10. A 5 volt arduino and a power load of 12 volts powers this circuit. The 5 volt arduino powers the photoresitor and the 12 volt power load powers the load of the LED strip. If the photoresistor senses an output value greater than 20 then the LED strip will turn off and if the value is greater than 10 the LED strip will light up with a value of 220.
